Welcome to CaPTk!

This application was developed at CBICA (Center for Biomedical Image Computing & Analytics). It is based on the well established, free open source Medical Imaging Interaction Toolkit MITK.

Our application is still in transition from the old, non-MITK based, version. Thus, we do not offer any tutorials yet. Here you can find the "Welcome Tutorial" and further information from MITK, to get a first overview. For the old non-MITK version of CaPTk click here.
